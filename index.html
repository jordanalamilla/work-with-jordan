Hi, I’m Jordan Alamilla and I specialize in creating custom portfolio sites for artists. My first priority is capturing your unique style and using my abilities to show the world who you are.

Being an artist myself, I know how difficult it can be to express your style with predefined blog themes and website builders.

With my knowledge and experience, I can build a website, tailored specifically to your vision.

Planning

Email, phone call, video call, in-person meetings, whatever works for you, I begin by gathering requirements and getting an idea of your style and who you are, using a series of interview questions.

Design

Using the information gathered, I create a series of possible designs. This phase is totally open to your input. Only once you’re happy with a final design and list of features, will I move on to the code.

Code

I’m a full stack developer, meaning I can build a complete website on my own, from a basic brochure style website to a full web application with your own login and dashboard to add, edit and delete art pieces.

Custom Graphics

With knowledge of graphic design, I can take your physical art pieces and turn them into digital image files, customizable in any way imaginable and optimized for your site to give it that much more personality.

Contact Me

Even if you’re not an artist or don't have a vision, I can help. Send me a message and let’s figure it out.
